Introduction

Oooit Art was established in the Netherlands in January 2007 to support promising visual artists; to promote art and cultural exchange between countries. Oooit Art also assists art collectors and art lovers in finding their way in the international art world.

Oooit Art has three main focus areas:

  • ART EXHIBITIONS
  • International AiR Projects
  • Art collection services

International AiR Project

{% include slider2.html %}

Oooit Art assists and facilitates young and emerging artists with their international AiR (Artist in Residence) projects. In each AiR project, Oooit Art co-operates with local partners to provide studios, boarding facilities, exhibition opportunities, cultural exchange programs, career consultancy, etc.

Oooit Art started International AiR program in the following locations:

  • Beijing, China
  • Shenzhen, China
  • Wuhan, China
  • Amsterdam, The Netherlands
  • Alkmaar, The Netherlands

An Oooit Art exchange program covers a period from one to three months.

Application: three months in advance. (Apply with working plan, CV and art work photos)

Enrollment: twee weeks for confirmation.

Art disciplines include: painting, sculpture/installation, photography, video, ceramic, graphic prints, etc.

Art collection services

{% include slider3.html %}

Oooit Art offers tailor made services to both private and institutional collectors. All art works are authenticated, certified and guaranteed by established professionals.

Please feel free to contact Oooit Art’s professionals providing expertise in:

  • Chinese contemporary art
  • Dutch contemporary art
  • German contemporary art
  • 17th Century Dutch and Flemish masters
  • 19th Century masters
  • 20th Century masters

Lectures or master courses may be arranged at relevant locations (e.g. museum, art fair, auction house, gallery etc.) for interested parties. On demand certificates are provided by several cooperating European universities. Studies of the following topics are available:

  • Artistic appreciation
  • Art history
  • Art valuation
  • Art finance
  • International art market
